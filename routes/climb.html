Route Name: D.U.MB.B.O

Location: David Thompson Corridor - Scary Slabs Area

YDS Grade: 5.7 - 3 Pitches

Description: Park on roadside turnout, approx 300m after you pass Windy Point ( heading West) there should be flagging indicating the way. Head up, treanding climbers right as you head towards a large slabby outcropping. The base of the climb starts by a burned out tree.

Pitch 1: Only a couple tricky slab moves, otherwise a great warm up pitch.
Pitch 2: Traversing over some cool rocks, you move up one tricky section to more stable ground.
Pitch 3: A short move or two of vertical climbing followed by some super fun cruisy slab!
Walkoff: Head climbers right after completing Pitch 3, and after a hundred meters or so you will join up with a hiking Trail which will lead you back to the Windy point parking lot.
